<blockquote class="review negative">
<h4>Trin <time datetime="2026-05-31">31/05/2026</time></h4>
Overall a disappointing remaster. They did not make full utilization of existing features from other PvZ ports, such as achievements, gamemodes, etc. leaving the full game to feel incomplete.

There are a number of frustrating bugs and performance issues that do not exist in the original version of the game. If you can live without the quality of life improvements (upscaled art, fast-forward button on levels, and some added gamemodes) then I would recommend just playing the GOTY version for a fifth of the price.

If you like PvZ, you will like it. But unless you are extremely eager for new content, it’s not really worth it.

4. Dancing zombie doesn't spawn backup dancers as often making him completely useless in iZombie endless
