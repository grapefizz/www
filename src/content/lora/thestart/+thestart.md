## google dev fest

### day 1

It all started on 30/10/2025, in Lisbon. I was eating dinner with my family in a small local restaurant and I saw an insta post
about some google devfest, I'd heard about it from last year so I thought I'd rsvp for this year. Little did I know it'd be the best
decision of my entire life.

Fast forward to 05/12/2025 at 2:15 pm. I walk into the venue and saw a gorgeous nerdy girl standing at the reception. I took a few steps inside
and she waved at me!!!! Even though it was really cute I kinda just walked away and didn't wave back (I'm still mad at myself for not waving back).
I then realized I was almost an hour early for the event so I went back home and came back at 3:00 pm. I sat down near the stage and listened
to the speaker talk about their life at google. Another speaker finished after him and back at the reception I see a friend of mine I hadn't
seen in a while. I walk up to him and greet him and right next to him is the girl that waved at me at the start. I'm absolutely overwhelmed 
with joy and excitement. In my mind she was the reason I came to the reception in the first place. Her pretty eyes, luscious hair and friendly
and warm smile made me absolutely fall for her the instant I saw her. My friend bent down and pulled a bag of stickers from his backpack and
laid them on the table. We were all looking at them and picked a few to put on my laptop as it was a fresh blank slate. A few moments later
one of the event organizers called my friend to help and I was ended up alone with this beautiful girl, so of course I struck up a conversation.
We talked until the next speaker came up to the stage and right before I left I asked her for her insta. Looking back on it I have no clue
where I got the confidence to ask her. She happily gave me her insta and I sat back near the front. After every speech I'd look back at the
reception to see if she was there. Every time I'd see her I'd waddle over and talk to her.

The event wrapped up and I stayed a bit longer since I wanted to spend more time with her. All the guests left and it was just me, her and a
few other volunteers. We all sat down and started sharing stories. After that was done everyone left and it was just my friend, her, and I.
We left the venue last and walked around for a bit while talking.

### day 2

Second day of the event I didn't really feel like going but I pushed myself to go since I knew she'd be there. I got to the event a little
bit early and came to find out she wouldn't come until later in the day. This saddened me a bit but knowing she'd swing by later made me feel
better. Fast forward to when she came at about 4 pm and I was thrilled. I was sitting in the middle of the rearmost row of seats and when
she arrived she sat right next to me. After a few minutes she suggested we leave and go to a different place instead of the auditorium and
I agreed. We had an amazing time talking and eating some of the leftover sandwiches. After the event was over, me, her and 2 other volunteers
decided to go and play pool. We got there by bus and had an amazing time. I partnered up with her and after every ball she potted I wanted to hug
and squeeze her so much. I was completely head over heels. After a game of billiards we went to play some ping pong. While warming up
she didn't feel very well and went outside to get some fresh air, so I joined her. We sat on the stairs outside of the place and started
singing together. It sounds really cringy but it was a really wholesome moment I'll never forget. We ended up staying there for a solid half 
hour before the other two volunteers came out and joined us. We ended the day there and parted ways, it was an amazing day and I had lots of
fun.

### day 3

The event only lasted 2 days and so I thought I wouldn't see her for a few days, but luckily she invited me to go to a small work cafe and
study together there. It sounded like a wonderful idea and I decided to go like any person would. We met up at the city center and started walking
to the cafe. We got there and it turns out they were closed because it was a sunday. After a bit of discussion we decided to find another 
place to study. We started wandering around the city and while walking we were googling nearby work cafes. We found a nice one but it was a
few blocks away so we started walking. I purposely took a detour to make the walk longer so we could spend more time together. We finally made
it to the place and from the outside we saw that it was cozy but the tables were too small for our laptops. We ended up walking around even
more in search of a better place and decided to go to an ice cream shop and study there. I planned to study but we just ended up talking lol.
We then packed up after a few scoops of ice cream and spent the rest of the day walking around the city. When it got dark we went on top of
an abandoned building. We sat down and I built up the courage to put my arm around her. We sat there and sang "Just the Two of Us" and it
was one of the most wholesome and memorable moments of my life. It was absolutely adorable. After the song ended I softly asked her for a
hug. Looking back on it, it was kinda awkward, but I loved that hug. It felt right, it was everything it needed to be and more. Even though it's
just a hug, I'll never forget it. Soon after we had to part ways and I got one last hug before she left. One of the best days of my life.

So those were our first 3 days :> Some of the best memories I have ever had.
