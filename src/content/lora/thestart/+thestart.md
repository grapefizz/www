## google dev fest

### day 1

All started on 30/10/2025, in Lisbon. I was eating dinner with my family in a small local restaurant and I see an insta post
about some google dev fest, I'd heard about it from last year so I thought I'd rsvp for this year. Little did I know it'd be the best
decision of my entire life.

Fast forward to 05/12/2025 2:15 pm. I walk into the venue and see a gorgeous nerdy girl standing at the reception. I took a few steps inside
and the waved at me!!!! Even though it was really cute I kinda just walked away and didn't wave back (I'm still mad at myself for not waving back).
I then realized I was almost an hour early for the event so I went back home and came back at 3:00 pm. I sat down near the stage and listened
to the speaker talk about their life at google. Another speaker after him finishes and back at the reception I see a friend of mine I hand't
seen in a while. I walk up to him and greet him and right next to him is the girl that waved at me at the start. I'm absolutely overwhelmed 
with joy and excitement. In my mind she was the reason I came to the reception in the first place. Her pretth eyes, lucious hair and friendly
and warm smile made me absolutely fall for her the instant I saw her. My friend bent down and pulled a bag of stickers from hi backpack and
laid them on the table. We were all looking at them and picked a few to put on my laptop as it was a fresh blank slate.
